      <h2>What is <code>p5.Oscillator()</code>?</h2>

      <p>The <code>p5.Oscillator()</code> object lets you generate and control sounds in your browser. It's part of the <code>p5.sound</code> library and can produce sine, triangle, square, or sawtooth waves.</p>

      <h2>How Does it Work?</h2>

      <ul>

        <li><strong>Waveform:</strong> The shape of the sound wave (<code>sine</code>, <code>triangle</code>, <code>square</code>, <code>sawtooth</code>).</li>

        <li><strong>Frequency:</strong> How high or low the pitch is (Hz).</li>

        <li><strong>Amplitude:</strong> The volume (0.0 = silent, 1.0 = loudest).</li>

        <li><strong>Start/Stop:</strong> Play or pause the oscillator.</li>

      </ul>

      <p>Interact with the controls above to see how changing parameters affects the sound!</p>
