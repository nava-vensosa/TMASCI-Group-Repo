What is p5.Oscillator()?

The p5.Oscillator() object lets you generate and control sounds in your browser. It's part of the p5.sound library and can produce sine, triangle, square, or sawtooth waves.

Try It Yourself!

Below you can create and control an oscillator. Try changing the waveform, frequency, or amplitude, and start/stop the sound!

How Does it Work?

  • Waveform: The shape of the sound wave (sine, triangle, square, sawtooth).
  • Frequency: How high or low the pitch is (Hz).
  • Amplitude: The volume (0.0 = silent, 1.0 = loudest).
  • Start/Stop: Play or pause the oscillator.
